Lua

Remnant - RPG Game

Remnant is by far my largest project planned, currently being developed by me and one of my friends at the University of Detroit Mercy. With its creation in Roblox Studio using the Lua language, we aim to incorporate major MMORPG elements such as skill trees, player classes, and turn-based combat. While the game is still a work in progress, we plan to make significant advancements on Remnant throughout the year.
